About The Event

MIC Pitch Camp 2.0 will be an Intra MIST Presentation competition. Being an engineer most of the students face problem in MIST regarding presentation and attending in business competition or idea competition. Pitch Camp will be a similar competition for MISTians which will give them the ability to present their idea to the outside world and also develop his/her presentation skills.

Description of 1st round

The teams will select a topic from above and then they will give an innovative idea or solution regarding the topic. Then the team will make a video of the idea within 4-5 minutes. The video link needs to be submitted in the given link and must be visible to the MIC authority. We will upload the submitted video from our Facebook page.


Topics

Information and Technology

Cyber Harassment

A common phenomenon of misusing the internet and not focusing on the repercussions of free use, we will be aiming to solve this by implementing technical priorities.


Industrial Problem

The Fourth Industrial Revolution

It represents a fundamental change in the way we live, work and relate to one another, a new chapter in human development. Turning overpopulation with human resources for 4th Industrial Revolution will be a great use for us.


Environmental Problem

Alternatives of Plastics

In our plastic-filled world, avoiding plastic can be pretty challenging. We will be aiming to solve this by production of plastic by-products.


Education

Assurance of Quality Education in All Terrains

It provides the foundation for equity in society & one of the most basic public services. It not only enlightens but also empowers citizens.


Social Issues

Parenting - Upbringing and its effects

A child tends to embrace the very core of his/her individuality from the environment around him/her and the way he/she is being raised.


Health & Hygiene

Mental health

We are always concerned about physical health but we don't give any importance to our mental health. Currently, 34% of people are suffering from both anxiety and depressive disorder.
